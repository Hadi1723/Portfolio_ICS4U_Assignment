Top 10 Modules

1. Algorithms

This module was essential in my overall understanding of computer science as it taught me many important concepts such as recursion, sorting algorithms, and graph theory. I was already introduced to algorithms from the Algorithms Exploration assignment which explored the connections between cubing and computer science. I found that learning to solve Rubik's cubes and Pyraminxes helped further develop my understanding of algorithms as I had a physical object to visualize this concept, so clearly cubing has had a significant impact on me as a student. I will continue my exploration of algorithms by learning and mastering more and more challenging cubes to better understand algorithms and to increase my efficiency.
